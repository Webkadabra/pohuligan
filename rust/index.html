Pohuligan is Hardcore Rust Vanilla server

Server Info

Pohuligan is mostly Vanilla Rust, with lowered upkeep cost and custom balance tailored for an authentic hardcore survival experience:

  • Sleeping Bags have a bigger timer reset range, to prevent death spamming. Stay alive!
  • Increased hostility timers - if you decide to attack, you stay out of the Safe Zones. Stay alert!
  • Plants grow slower. Protect your weed!
  • No free airdrops or locked crates if there too few people online
  • Team limit of 3
  • Discord: https://discord.gg/TvZtdBpr
  • No other changes to the gameplay
