Pohuligan is HArdcore Rust Vanilla server

Server Info

Pohuligan is mostly Vanilla Rust, with custom balance tailored for an authentic hardcore survival experience:

  • Sleeping Bags reset range is way bigger, to prevent death spamming
  • Increased hostility timers - you wanna kill, you stay out of the Safe Zones
  • Plants grow slower - protect your weed!
  • Team limit of 3
